About

I am Wilson Udomisor, and I use data science to answer very practical questions. How is performance trending. Which actions really move the needle. Where are the hidden risks and opportunities in your data. My goal is to bring structure and clarity to messy information so that your next move feels obvious and well supported.

I work across the full data science lifecycle. I explore raw data, clean it, and model it with Python, SQL and Pandas. I use Scikit learn to build straightforward models that are transparent and easy to explain. I design tables and transformations so that dashboards in Power BI or other tools show exactly the metrics you care about, with no confusion about how the numbers were created.

This portfolio focuses on real world style projects, not toy examples. You will see batch analytics that track demand and revenue over time, streaming style simulation that reacts as events arrive, and analytics engineering work that builds clean and tested layers on top of warehouses. In each project I explain the problem, the choices I made, the trade offs, and what the results mean for you.

I enjoy breaking complex ideas into simple pieces that non technical stakeholders can follow. That means clear documentation, readable code, and a strong story for every chart and table. If you are looking for someone who can sit between business and technology, ask the right questions, and then turn those questions into working data science solutions, this portfolio is my invitation to you.
