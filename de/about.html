About

I am Wilson Udomisor, a data engineer focused on building systems that are simple to run, easy to trust, and ready to scale. I care about the full lifecycle of data, from ingestion and quality checks to transformation, orchestration, and delivery into analytics ready layers.

In this Data Engineering portfolio, I present three connected projects that tell a complete story. The platform baseline standardises local infrastructure and makes development reproducible. The batch medallion pipeline uses Spark, Airflow, and dbt to move data from Bronze to Silver to Gold in a Postgres warehouse with clear testing and documentation. The real time risk pipeline uses Kafka for event streaming and Flink for continuous processing, with Airflow supporting governance and scheduling. A daily Spark reconciliation job helps keep streaming outputs aligned with batch truth.

My goal is to help teams reduce pipeline failures, detect issues early, and ship metrics with confidence. If you need someone who can translate requirements into clean pipelines, strong documentation, and working demos, you are in the right place.
