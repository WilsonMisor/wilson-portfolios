About Wilson Sunday Udomisor

I am Wilson Sunday Udomisor, a data engineer and data scientist focused on building systems that are simple to run, easy to trust, and ready to scale. I care about the full lifecycle of data—from ingestion and quality checks to transformation, orchestration, modeling, and delivery into analytics-ready layers and actionable insights.

My work bridges two worlds. As a data engineer, I build reliable batch and real-time pipelines using Spark, Airflow, Kafka, dbt, and Postgres. I design platform infrastructure, implement medallion architectures (Bronze, Silver, Gold), and ensure data flows are reproducible, well-tested, and documented. As a data scientist, I focus on practical, explainable solutions—demand forecasting, fraud detection, analytics engineering, and BI dashboards—using Python, SQL, Pandas, Scikit-learn, and Power BI.

This portfolio presents six connected projects that tell a complete story across both disciplines. You will see platform design, batch medallion pipelines, real-time streaming systems, retail demand forecasting, fraud and risk monitoring, and modern analytics engineering with dbt. Each project includes Docker Compose demos, diagrams, clear READMEs, and working code that demonstrates how I design, orchestrate, transform, validate, and analyze data end to end.

My goal is to help teams reduce pipeline failures, detect issues early, ship metrics with confidence, and turn data into decisions that stakeholders can trust. If you need someone who can translate requirements into clean pipelines, transparent models, strong documentation, and working demos, you are in the right place.
